FRO3 is a simple and fast Rule of Three calculator.

It helps calculate unknown values using direct or inverse proportions. Enter three values and FRO3 calculates the missing value instantly.

Features:
• Direct and inverse proportion calculations
• Material 3 interface with light and dark themes
• Dynamic colors support
• English and Portuguese translations
• Locale-aware decimal formatting
• Copy and share calculation results
• Desktop support with responsive layout

The app works completely offline and does not require an account or internet connection.
